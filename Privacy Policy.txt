Privacy Policy
Effective Date: September 5, 2024
Issued by: Bitcoin Silver Team
Contact: info@bitcoinsilver.top
We, the creators of the Bitcoin Silver (BTCS) Wallet, are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data. By using our app, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Data Collection and Processing
• 	We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it.
• 	The BTCS Wallet operates without requiring registration and does not request sensitive data such as your name, address, or email.
• 	However, certain data may be automatically collected during app usage:
• Device Information: Includes device type, operating system, and app version.
• Transaction Data: Public blockchain details such as sending/receiving addresses and transaction amounts.

2. Use of Collected Data
• 	Automatically collected data is used solely to ensure the wallet functions properly.
• 	We do not store personal data on our servers.

3. Sharing Data with Third Parties
• 	We do not share personal data with third parties.
• 	While blockchain transactions are publicly visible, we do not store or track this information.

4. Data Security
• 	Your privacy is our top priority.
• 	Private keys and sensitive data remain stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

5. User Rights
• 	Since we do not retain personal data, you may delete the app at any time without leaving any trace.
• 	If our policy changes, you will be notified and given the opportunity to access, modify, or delete your data.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy
• 	We may update this policy to reflect legal or technical changes.
• 	The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website.

7. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us at:
